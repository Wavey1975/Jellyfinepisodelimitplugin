🌙 Episode Limit

Stop playback after X episodes — perfect for falling asleep!

How to Use

1

Start watching a show

Play any TV episode as normal

2

Tap to show controls

Tap the screen to reveal the player controls

3

Tap the moon/bedtime icon 🌙

Look for the bedtime icon in the control bar

4

Choose your limit

Pick 1-5 with the quick buttons, or enter a custom number up to 100

5

Sleep easy!

The icon turns green. After your episodes finish, playback stops automatically.

⚡ One-Time Setup (30 seconds)

To show the button in the player, add this to Dashboard → General → Custom JavaScript:

/* Episode Limit Plugin */ (function(){var s=document.createElement('script');s.src='/EpisodeLimit/episodelimit.js';s.async=true;document.head.appendChild(s);})();

After adding, click Save and refresh your browser. The 🌙 button will appear in the player!

Works with Any Media

The plugin counts completed files, not time. So it works with everything!

Note: The limit resets after it triggers. You'll set it fresh each night — this way you're always in control and it won't surprise you by stopping when you don't expect it!
